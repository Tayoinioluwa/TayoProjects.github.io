DIVERSITY & EQUALITY ANALYSIS

I adopted the use of power BI Analytical tools to generate insights and recomendations for ACME Corporations after diagnosing data from the HR department.

Acme Corporation is committed to creating a fair and equitable workplace for all employees, regardless of race, ethnicity, gender, age, marital status, or education level. However, my task was to help the company recognize some areas where it can improve.

Recomendations.

Consider a balance in diversity during our next recruitment drive.

Transfer of current staff from branches with excess similar race to ensure mix-race in branches.

Ensure equal opportunity to all gender in middle salary level.

Effective trainings on cultural values and diversity.

Staff engagement programs i.e., celebrating cultural heritages (Cross cultural programs).

RECRUITMENT TRACKING ANALYSIS

Insights.

Software Engineers and Data Analyst takes longer time to fill.
